TRADITION

What is Veuve Clicquot?

Veuve Clicquot stands not only for champagne but as a cultural entity spanning generations. The brand is intertwined with endurance, pushing boundaries and championing modern artistry. For racers at prestigious events like 24h Le Mans, spraying the vibrant bottle is the ultimate crescendo, representing not just a win, but historical lineage. It echoes tradition, luxury, and the beauty of resilience across decades of World Heritage and relentless competition.

The Palette

The signature Clicquot Yellow creates an unmistakable identity, complementing the iconic Porsche crest. The design language speaks to a precise aesthetic choice: warm hues, metallic sheen, and raw engineering forms merging into visual excellence. The interplay showcases the perfect contrast between historic elegance and raw motorsport typography on the asphalt.
